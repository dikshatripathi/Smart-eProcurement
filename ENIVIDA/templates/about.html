About Us

Smart eTender(SeT) is an online solution to conduct all stages of a tender process. SeT converts tedious procurement process into an economical, transparent and more secure system.

Our Vision

To be the most efficient and smart automate portal for AICTE eprocurement. A portal that will make the tender process, innovative and easy to use and access.

Our Mission

To provide consistent and intelligent tender lists. To automate the processofo choosing best and suitable tenderer/bidder. To make the approval process easy and smart.

Benefits of Smart eTender (SeT)


For Tender Inviting Authorities (TIA)

  • Easy to create and publish tender
  • Approvals updated at each stage
  • Fast and efficient process
  • Helps to choose the best bidder
  • Reduce the tender approvals time

For Bidders

  • Information available on 24x7
  • Automated system - cuts down on logistical & stationary cost
  • Bidder gets better access to all tenders
  • SeT has brought in confidence among the bidders as being fair & transparent
